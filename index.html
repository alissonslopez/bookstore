BookVault

What is BookVault?

This is a simple book list tool where you can add or delete books of your choice. It’s open, no login, and no approval needed.

  • Add your favorites
  • Write about it (genre, total pages, how it made you feel)
  • or clear the clutter!

    • URL image wont load

  • Choose whichever picture you want based on the book
  • Right-click the photo
  • Select Open image to new tab.
  • Then copy and past the URL

Quick Start

  1. Click Take me to the Books.
  2. Use the form to add a book.
  3. Use the trash icon to delete one.
